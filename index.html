Wind Harmony is a tool for simulating the sound of wind, but with the properties of musical intervals and scales. Using the keyboard, you can activate the notes as if they were piano keys, to get a menacing, sad, happy wind, etc.

The controls allow you to:
- Modulate the amplitude (Amp.), frequency (Freq.) and bandwidth (Q) of the oscillator.
- Go up or down an octave (keys Z and X).
- Keep the notes active (C).
- Record and download an audio file.

* App only available for desktop (for now).
